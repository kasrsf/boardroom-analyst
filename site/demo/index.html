Answer

March revenue growth slowed because brand video declined while shopping and visual search continued to grow. Total ad revenue still rose from $925M to $973M, but the monthly lift stepped down from +$69M to +$48M.

Decision lens

Ask the monetization team to separate brand video demand softness from campaign delivery or pricing effects. The documented mart supports month x surface analysis; it does not yet support advertiser cohort causality.

Caveats

All numbers come from synthetic MVP data. Claims are restricted to documented model grain and source query IDs q001 and q002.

q001_surface_revenue
select month, surface, net_revenue_millions
from ad_revenue_by_surface
order by month, surface;

q002_revenue_growth_waterfall
with totals as (
  select month, sum(net_revenue_millions) as total_revenue_millions
  from ad_revenue_by_surface
  group by 1
)
select month, total_revenue_millions,
       total_revenue_millions - lag(total_revenue_millions) over (order by month) as revenue_change_millions
from totals
order by month;
